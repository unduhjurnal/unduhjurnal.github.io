14. 6.Rearrange these words into good sentences!Some – they – in – classroom – make – bottles - blueA. They some make bottles blue in classroomB. They make some bottles blue in classroomIn classroom they make blue some bottlesD. They make some blue bottles in classroomC.they make some blue bottles in Classroom​


Answer:d. they make some blue bottles in classroom

—

Discussion:

Diperintahkan untuk merangkai rapi kata-kata di atas yang berupa kalimat acak, maka agar kalimatnya menjadi kalimat yang berlangsung dengan baik—hal pertama adalah perhatikan bentuk dari masing-masing katanya dan akan menjadi kalimat apa.

Berikut struktur dari masing-masing kata:

some: adverbthey: pronounin: prepositionclassroom: nounmake: verbbottles: nounblue: adjective

Kalimatnya menjadi simple present karena adanya verba make

S + V1(-s/es) + ...

karena verbanya make maka subyeknya they dan kata ganti they bersifat subjective

—> they make...

setelah verba, lengkapi dengan nounclassroom dilengkapi in menjadi in classroom karena in bottles dianggap tidak sesuai dengan konteks kalimatnya sehingga menjadi adverb of place

—> they make ... in classroom.

blue adalah rincian dari suatu obyek dimana obyek yang dimaksud adalah bottles sehingga berpola adjective + noun menjadi blue bottles

—> they make blue bottles in classroom

untuk menyatakan jumlah diprakirakan tidak banyak/sedikit/beberapa, some diletakkan setelah noun

—> jawabannya:

they make some blue bottles in classroom

—> opsi: d.

26. why must discourse and pragmatics? why not discourse and semantics? ​


Why does it have to be discourse and pragmatics?

why not discourse and semantics?

-----------------------

Answer

Because discourse and pragmatics deal with meaning in context.

-----------------------

Explanation

In linguistics, discourse refers to the study of verbal or written language in a communicative context. Discourse is the way language is used to convey its meaning. This meaning is dictated by the social conditions, the speaker of the language and the conditions at the time it is spoken.

Semantics in linguistics is the study of words and their meanings.

Pragmatics in linguistics is also the study of words and their meanings but with emphasize in the context in which they are used.

Semantics focuses on the literal meaning of the words while pragmatics focuses on the literal and inferred meanings and also the context, such as deixis, turns in the conversation, and text organisation.

So it can be concluded that why discourse is more intertwined with pragmatics than with semantics because both discourse and pragmatics deal with meaning in context, not just the literal meaning.

---------------
