11. 3. What is the divination of: a. Narrative text b. Descriptive text c. Recount text d. Report text e. Procedure text​


Jawaban:

a. Narrative text: Narrative text is a form of writing that tells a story. It typically includes characters, a setting, a plot, and a resolution.

b. Descriptive text: Descriptive text is a form of writing that uses words to paint a vivid picture of a person, place, or thing. It allows the reader to see, hear, smell, taste, and feel what is being described.

c. Recount text: Recount text is a form of writing that recounts an event or experience. It typically includes setting, characters, a climax, and a resolution.

d. Report text: Report text is a form of writing that conveys information to the reader. It typically includes facts, figures, and analysis.

e. Procedure text: Procedure text is a form of writing that explains how to do something. It typically includes steps, materials, and safety precautions.

33. perbedaan descriptive teks, report teks, recoun teks, narrative teks dan procedure teks


1. Descriptive text : salah satu teks essay yang berfungsi untuk mendiskripsikan seseorang, seekor hewan, atau sebuah tempat secara spesifik, dan menggunakan simple present tense
2. Report text : sama dengan descriptive text, tetapi mendiskripsi secara umum
3. Recount text : salah satu teks essay yang berfungsi untuk menceritakan kembali pengalaman seseorang dan menggunakan simple past tense
4. Narrative text : salah satu teks yang berfungsi sebagai hiburan dan menggunakan simple past tense
5. Procedure text : salah satu teks essay yang berfungsi untuk memberithau cara untuk melakukan suatu hal dan menggunakan simple present tense
