20. Mention the types of personal letters!​


Jawaban:

types of personal letters are:

1. Family letter: Written to family members, the purpose is to share recent news among them.

2. Condolence letter: Written letter to a friend to show sympathy, usually for the recipient. You might wish them strength through their grief, say your fond memories of the person they have lost.

3. Holiday/Celebration letter: A letter which is sent during the holiday or celebration such as new year, eid-mubarak, christmas, etc.

4. Farewell letter: ‘A goodbye letter’ for your friends or family who is moving to other city or abroad.

5. Congratulations letter: A written form to say congratulations for someone’s achievement.

! -› Faktorial

2. Cover letter separated from curriculum vitae. In this way the curriculum vitae is an attachment and this method is called a separate model.
