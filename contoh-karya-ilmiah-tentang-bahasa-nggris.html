10. bantu jwb solatifi nggris kelas 8 halaman 54​


Jawaban:

1. Would you like to show me how to send a fax?

The correct answer is c. asking one's understanding.

This question is asking for clarification or confirmation of the person's willingness to demonstrate how to send a fax.

2. The staff said, "Sure."

The correct answer is a. No problem.

The staff's response "Sure" indicates that they are willing and able to show the guest how to send a fax, similar to saying "No problem."

3. An expression of inviting someone to do something is shown by utterance

The correct answer is c. How about having a drink?

In this dialog, Reza is inviting Riza to do something by suggesting having a drink together.

4. A positive response to one's invitation is shown by utterance...

The correct answer is d. Sure, l'd love to!

Riza's response "Sure, I'd love to!" indicates that she positively accepts the invitation to have a drink.

5. The suitable utterance to complete the dialog is ...

The correct answer is b. Let's go to the new restaurant at lunchtime!

Hari is suggesting going to a new restaurant, so the most suitable response would be to propose going to that restaurant at lunchtime.

6. The suitable utterance to complete the dialog is ...

The correct answer is b. Well, l'd love to, but l've already been there a few days ago.

Aini's response indicates that she would love to go to the restaurant, but she has already been there recently.

better? Is there any progress with your
condition?
Patient : Yes sir, I am feeling better. But
sometimes I really feel weary, whereas I feel
really healthy before that. Is it normal or not
doctor?
Doctor : Yes that is really normal. That symptom
indicates you will be healthy. May I check your
body temperature and blood pressure please?
Patient : Yes, of course doctor.
Doctor : After I checked, your body temperature
is 38o C and your blood pressure is 120/ 80. I can
diagnose that your healthy is getting better, your
blood pressure has been normal, but your body
temperature is still high. You need to have more
rest to make it better.
Patient : How about the medicine sir? I have to
consume the new medicine or the previous
medicine?
Doctor : You have still to consume the previous
one. The nurse will give the medicine that I
recommend. By the way, how’s about the service
here? Is there any problem about that?
Patient : I am satisfied about the service. The
nurse is very friendly and the facility is very
well. They also keep my room sanitation. So I am
very comfort being cared here.
Doctor : Okay I have to visit the other patient, if
there is complaint about your condition. Just call
me. I will come to your room. Get well soon and
good morning.
Patient : Ok doctor. Thank you for checking me
today. Good morning.

26. puisi bahasa i nggris tertema laut


A Day At Ssea

As the ocean waves at me,

and the sand greets the sea,

the fish swim free,

and shells wash up by me.

the sand squishes suddenly,

between my shoeless toes.

then the flows over them,

and back down it goes.

the salt is on my tongue,

the sea's song is sung,

the sun is going down,

and so my day at sea is done
