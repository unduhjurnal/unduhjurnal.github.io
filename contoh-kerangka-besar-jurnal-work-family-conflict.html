Sampit Tragedy

This tragedy originated from a conflict between the Dayak and Madura ethnic groups that took place in Sampit, Central Kalimantan. Tempo noted that the conflict began on February 18, 2001 when four Madurese family members, Matayo, Haris, Kama and his wife, were killed. The Madurese then went to the house belonging to the Dayak tribe named Timil who was considered to have hidden the murderer. The crowd requested that Timil surrender the perpetrators of the murder. Because their request was not followed, the mob was angry and burned the house. That night's incident could be stopped by the police. Unfortunately, the burning continued to spread to other houses.

Dayak residents from Sampit began arriving, both by land and river. Ethnic Madurese are chased and killed. Natives seem to know where the pockets of Madurese are. Young men and women are subject to murder. On several roads, the body of the victim's body was headless.

Most of the ethnic Madurese must be evacuated to East Java and Central Java. The casualties increased and there were already no counting how many houses and public facilities were burned. It is estimated that fatalities reached 469 in the 10-day conflict.

2. Maluku Conflict

This conflict is a violent conflict with a background of religious differences between Islam and Christian groups. Maluku conflict is called the highest number of victims, which is around 8-9 thousand people killed. In addition, more than 29 thousand houses were burned, and 45 mosques, 47 churches, 719 shops, 38 government buildings, and 4 banks were destroyed. The range of conflicts that occur is also the longest, namely up to 4 years.

3. Conflict 1998

The economic crisis led to a social conflict at the end of the New Order. The fall of Suharto was marked by widespread riots in various regions of Indonesia. During the riots, many shops and companies were destroyed by angry mobs. The main target is property belonging to ethnic Chinese.

Chinese descendants even became victims of abuse and rape during the riots. Many were raped together, persecuted, and then killed. Among the ethnic Chinese, many left Indonesia to seek safety.

The United Nations has designated 21 May as the Day of Dialogue and Diversity since 2002. Today's commemoration begins when UNESCO issued the Universal Declaration on Cultural Diversity. Through Resolution Number 57/249, 21 May was established as a day to celebrate diversity throughout the world.

The United Nations records as many as 75 percent of the major conflicts that occur in the world today are rooted in the cultural dimension. The UN also launched a dialogue to bridge culture in order to create peace. Simple actions suggested by the UN to celebrate cultural diversity include visiting cultural exhibitions, listening to music from different cultures, inviting neighbors of different religions or tribes to eat together, or watching films that revolve around different cultures.

13. Pada perilaku saluran distribusi, dapat saja terjadi conflict diantara perusahan – perusahaan yang terlibat, jelaskan dan berikan contohnya


Jawaban:

Konflik saluran horizontal dapat terjadi antar pengecer. Misalnya, ketika salah satu pengecer merasa ada pesaing lain yang bersaing terlalu ketat melalui penetapan harga atau menggunakan iklan untuk menyerang wilayah penjualan mereka. Konflik vertikal terjadi antara pengecer dan pemasok mereka ketika kedua pihak merasa didominasi oleh yang lain. Konflik dapat timbul ketika pabrikan membuat saluran baru.

Sumber umum lainnya konflik saluran adalah ketika saluran distribusi tradisional menjadi semakin ditantang oleh saluran distribusi alternatif yang baru. Contohnya adalah antara saluran online dan saluran konvensional.

20. apa yang dimaksud dengan etnopolitic conflict?


Masalah integrasi dan sparatisme dalam negara kesatuan yang multietnik dan struktur masyarakatnya majemuk, seperti "srigala berbulu domba" atau penuh ambivalensi (ambigu). Perfomancenya menampakan sebuah keseimbangan (equillibrium) diantara struktur sosial, politik, dan kebudayaannya, tetapi isinya penuh dengan intrik, ketidakpuasan, paradoks, etnosentrisme, stereotipisme, dan konflik sosial yang tidak kunjung selesai.

(?) Do you always work hard to earn money for your family?

Penjelasan:

Yang ditanyakan itu dalam negative form, jadi ada kata don't/doesn't/not/to be + not. Jika ingin diubah ke positive form (+), hilangkan kata not (semisal: don't -> do). Kata do/does bisa juga dihilangkan. Jika ingin diubah ke question, sama seperti ke positive form, tapi do/does/to be nya taruh didepan (sebelum subjeknya).
