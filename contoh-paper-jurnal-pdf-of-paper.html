4. prosedur text of paper plane​


Jawaban:

1.Fold the paper in half vertically.

2.Unfold the paper and fold each of the top corners into the center line.

3.Fold the top edges into the center line.

4.Fold the plane in half toward you.

5.Fold the wings down, matching the top edges up with the bottom edge of the body.

6.Add double stick tape to the inside of the body. The finished plane should look like this.

Jawaban:

Materials:

- scissors

- glue

- paper/refill

Steps:

1. First of all, prepare the material needed.

2. Second, cut the margin of the paper.

3. Then, fold the paper in half lengthways.

4. Next, fold a corner back so that the top edge touches the bottom edge and the folded corner is shaped like a triangle.

5. After that, proceed to do the same to the opposite corner.

6. Sixth,fold the whole top edge over

7. Next, proceed to do the same to the other side.

8. After that, open the wings back out so they stick out to the side

9. Then, open up the space between the wings.Put some glue in the middle

10. Finally, test fly the paper plane.

35. Jerry has a stack of coloured paper. 5 of the sheets of paper are blue. 8 30% of the remaining sheets of paper are pink. The other 84 sheets of paper are green. How many sheets of paper are in the stack altogether?​


Jawaban:

97 sheets of paper

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Let's start by finding the number of pink sheets:

Jerry has 5 blue sheets.

The remaining sheets are (total - blue) = (x - 5) sheets.

30% of the remaining sheets are pink, so the number of pink sheets is 0.3(x - 5).

We can write this as an equation:

0.3(x - 5) = 8

Solving for x:

0.3x - 1.5 = 8

0.3x = 9.5

x = 31.67 (rounded to 32)

So Jerry has a total of x sheets of paper, which is approximately 32.

Now we can check our work:

Jerry has 5 blue sheets.

The remaining sheets are (32 - 5) = 27 sheets.

30% of the remaining sheets are pink, which is 0.3 * 27 = 8.1 (rounded to 8) sheets.

The other 84 sheets are green.

Altogether, Jerry has 5 + 8 + 84 = 97 sheets of paper.

Therefore, there are 97 sheets of paper in the stack altogether.
