3. dapat menuliskan teks recount sangat pendek tentang pengalaman pribadi dengan judul my vacation last semester​


Dalam penulisan teks bahasa inggris, kita harus memahami berbagai jenis teks, salah satunya adalah recount. Untuk lebih jelasnya mari disimak pembahasan dan contoh penulisan recount text di bawah ini.

Pembahasan  

Recount text adalah teks yang mengandung tulisan tentang suatu peristiwa atau kejadian yang sudah terjadi di masa lalu. Ciri yang paling menonjol dari recount text ini adalah menjabarkan urutan peristiwa berdasarkan waktu setiap peristiwa tersebut. Recount text ini bertujuan untuk memberikan informasi dan atau menghibur pembaca akan sesuatu yang ditulis kembali oleh penulis.

Pada umumnya recount text terdiri dari 3 jenis, yaitu:

Personal recount

Personal recount adalah  jenis recount text yang menceritakan pengalaman pribadi penulis yang biasanya bertujuan untuk menghibur pembaca sekaligus memberikan informasi, apabila tulisan tersebut mengandung keterangan tentang kegiatan yang dialami penulis.

Factual recount

Factual recount adalah jenis recount text yang menceritakan kembali peristiwa yang benar-benar terjadi, seperti peristiwa sejarah, peristiwa bencana alam atau biografi.

Imaginative

Imaginative adalah jenis recount text yang menceritakan sebuah cerita karangan yang didalamnya terdapat peristiwa atau kejadian yang telah terjadi.

GENERIC STRUCTURE OF RECOUNT TEXT  (Susunan alur cerita pada recount text)

Orientation

Susunan alur cerita  ini ada pada paragraf atau kalimat pertama, penulis menjelaskan informasi mengenai di mana, kapan peristiwa atau kejadian itu terjadi dan siapa yang melakukannya.

Events

Susunan alur cerita berikutnya adalah events yang menjabarkan kejadian atau peristiwa yang terjadi secara berututan. Dan umumnya memakai chronological term, Seperti; First day, next day, second, dan lain sebagainya

Reorientation

Susunan alur cerita yang terakhir ini merupakan pengulangan kalimat yang mendeskripikan keseluruhan inti peristiwa yang sudah dujabarkan ada di awal cerita. Penulis juga dapat menambahkan komentar atau kesan personal terhadap peristiwa atau kejadian tersebut.

CIRI-CIRI RECOUNT TEXT

Menggunakan kalimat past tense, contoh : I went to Bali last year.Menggunakan action verbs  in past tense, contoh : Menggunakan adverb dan adverbial phrase untuk mengungkapkan waktu, tempat dan cara. Contoh : last month, in Bali, lately, dan lain sebagainya.Menggunakan conjunction dan time connectives untuk mengurutkan peristiwa. Contoh : and, or, after that, dan lain sebagainya.

nah, setelah memahami tata cara penulisan recount text, mari kita buat contoh recount text dengan menerapkan apa yang sudah kita pelajari diatas.

At The Seaside

Last semester, I and Habib and his family went to the Yogyakarta seaside for our summer holidays. We left the city very early in the morning on the 5th of July. The weather was not very fine and we wore our jackets because we felt cold. At 10 o'clock, the sun began to shine, and two hours later, when I step on the beach the sun was shining brightly. We took off our jackets and we all went to the same restaurant where we sat down to a good lunch. We ate fish and some very good mutton, and drank two bottles of excellent wine. After lunch, we went to the sea, which my little sister Hannah saw for the first time. We lay in the sun for two hours and then we went into the water which was very warm. When we came out of the water, the sun burnt us, so we sat down in the shade of some rocks. Habib lit a cigarette and I read the newspaper. Then we all went back to our lodging. Habib's mother wrote a few letters and postcards, whilst he and I played a game of billiards. After dinner, we went out and saw the sun set in a bright red sky. Then we went to bed, and slept very well.

The next morning, I awoke late, got up and had breakfast. My little sister wanted to go into the sea again, but the wind blew so hard that it was not possible to bathe. We went out, however, and stood and looked at the sea for some time. It was very beautiful. I told Hannah to hold her hat, but she held it only for a minute or two and then let it go. The wind blew it off, and away it went. A small boy brought it back to us and I gave him 5 thousand rupiahs. That was not all. When we were going back to the lodging. Hannah fell down, tore her dress and cut her hand. The day began badly for her. In the afternoon, the wind stopped and we went on the pier where we heard a very good concert. I met some friends and asked them to come with us for a walk along the beach. We went for a long walk and enjoyed very much. The end of the day was better than the beginning.

15. contoh recount teks yang pendek!


Recount text adalah teks yang menceritakan pengalaman penulis di waktu lampau (recount text is a text which retells the writer's past experience). Karena berbentuk lampau, maka tense yang digunakan adalah past tense. Dan karena menceritakan pengalaman penulis, kata ganti yang digunakan adalah kata ganti orang pertama tunggal (I, my) dan kata ganti orang pertama jamak (we).

Pembahasan

Contoh recount text pendek yaitu

Parangtritis Beach

         Last holiday, my family had a vacation. We went to Parangtritis beach It is located in the south of Yogyakarta city. We leaved at 05.30 a.m. and we arrived at 06.30 a.m. It was the time of sunrise.

         I saw sunrise and the wave of the Indian Ocean. I played a kite with my sister and  also made sand castle with my family. When I was tired, I sat on the sand and enjoyed the sea wind. At 11 a.m my family ate seafood in the food court around the beach. After lunch, we leaved the beach and went home. I felt very tired but I was happy. Because I could go with my family.

Contoh recount text pendek lainnya yaitu

         Last weekend we went camping at Bunder woods. We left for the woods early in the morning. After a long drive we arrived at Bunder woods and found a good camping site. "Let's set up our tent here," said our leader. The we started to work. We need two poles, some ropes, and pegs for the tent. In a short time the tent was ready. After that we put a small flag on the top of our tent.

It took around 7 hours to go to Palembang from Lampung by train. I arrived at Palembang at 2.30 P.M. I was free to do anything in this city because i didn’t plan to go certain places here except for Gelora Jakabaring Stadium. At first, I went directly to the stadium by bus. I saw so many people in the stadium. I just walked and sometimes stopped to buy merchandise around the stadium. I was there until 5 P.M. Being so tired and hungry, then i went to a hotel. I spent my first night there by taking a rest. I did many things and visited almost all of the momentous places along my journey there.

I really like Palembang which is also known as “The City of Empek-Empek”. That was my secound tour in Palembang. It was really memorable. Someday i want to go there again.

Writing Recount Text

Last semester, me and my family went to Tokyo, Japan for holiday. It was one of the unforgettable place I have ever visited.

Tokyo was not the first city abroad that I have visited, but surely it was the best so far. I enjoyed the entertainment the city offered, like Disneyland, Tokyo Tower and Meiji Jingu shrine.

I walked a lot there and surprisingly I enjoyed it. I could tell that the air was so fresh and the city was clean as well. People were really expressive with their style, particularly in the Harajuku area.

That was my story about my vacation last semester. Hopefully I could come back to Tokyo next time for another holiday.

Pembahasan

Recount text adalah sebuah bacaan yang memiliki menceritakan kembali kejadian atau pengalaman di masa lalu.

Struktur recount text:

Orientation - menceritakan awal mula kejadian dan siapa saja tokoh yang berperan juga tempat dan waktu kejadian.Events - runtutan peristiwa yang terdapat dalam alur cerita/pengalaman, biasanya terdiri atas beberapa bagian.Re-orientation - menceritakan tentang kesimpulan dan rangkuman cerita.

Tujuan: Menghibur dan memberikan informasi bagi para pembaca.

Karakteristik: Menggunakan past tense karena menceritakan pengalaman yang sudah terjadi.
